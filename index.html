Games starring cats!

We all love cats, am I right?, and we all love games too, am I right?, so what if we mix these two things together, cute cat games of all genres and for everyone, let's check out these games about cute cats doing cat things and some other non-cat things.

Catalog

My Idea:

I am pleased to bring you a collection of unique indie games starring cats. I believe in the fun, imagination and originality of indie games, and that is why I am dedicated to bring you some games with these ideas.

What I offer:

  • A Unique Collection: I've searched all corners of the indie gaming world to find titles that stand out for their creativity and love of cats.
  • Diversity of Genres: From adventure games to platformers and puzzles, I bring games for all tastes. If you love cats, we have something special for you!
  • A Community Corner: Not just a store, I share with you the discord server where you have a community where cat and video game lovers can connect, share stories and enjoy their passion together.

My Commitment:

I am proud to support independent developers who create games with love and dedication. Every game we offer has been selected based on the quality and love for cats that is reflected in them.
